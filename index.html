About

I am a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore. My work focuses on coastal protection and flood resilience, particularly the interaction between hydrodynamics, turbulence, sediment transport and coastal infrastructure.

I received my PhD from the University of Auckland, where I studied sediment entrainment mechanisms using smart sediment particles and hydraulic experiments.

Research interests

Local scour and sediment transport

Scour around bridges, monopiles, pipelines and coastal protection structures.

CFD and turbulence modelling

OpenFOAM modelling, stress omega turbulence closure and anisotropic suspended sediment diffusion.

Coastal hazard mitigation

Hybrid and nature based coastal protection, mangrove planter systems and wave attenuation.
